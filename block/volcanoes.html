Volcanoes!

Isabela Island, the largest islet in The Galapagos, is home to the Sierra Negra, an active volcano with giant tortoises. It rises to an altitude of 1124m and is a great opportunity to enjoy a long hike!

If you decide to travel here, be careful! In July 2018, the volcano was continously releasing lava and other volcanic products. Some highland residents were even evacuated and returned to their respective homes. This eruption has been controlled, however, the volcano is active!

Don't forget to visit the islands!
